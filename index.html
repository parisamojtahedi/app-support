The App offers in-app purchases and auto-renewable subscriptions to unlock premium features. All purchases are processed through Apple’s App Store. We do not store any credit card or billing information. Subscriptions can be managed via your Apple ID account settings.

You may control or limit data collection by adjusting app permissions or settings on your device. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device’s privacy settings.
